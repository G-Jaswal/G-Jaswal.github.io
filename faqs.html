Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Requested Information

Power Outlets

Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as the United States).

Alcohol Service

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 am.

Drinking Age

The drinking age on Tahiti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced.

Language

Many younger Tahiti residents speak fluent English. Very little English is spoke in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

Medical Services

There is on hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Safety

Violent crime is very rare on Tahiti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

National Holidays

Tahiti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

Currency

Tahiti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
