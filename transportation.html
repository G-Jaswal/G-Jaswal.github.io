Transportation

Almost all visitors arrive to Tahiti by air

Getting to Tahiti

By Air

Tahiti is served by a small airport that can accomodate small jets and propeller planes. Tahiti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

By Cruise Ship

Some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Ground Transportation

Public Buses

Public buses serve Tahiti City and run from 5:00 am to 11:00 pm every day.

Private Buses

Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis

Taxis are available in Tahiti City.

Rental Cars

Rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport.

Bikes

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors. Helmets are required by law.

Walking

Tahiti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing - this area is easy to explore on foot.
