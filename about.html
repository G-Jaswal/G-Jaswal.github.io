About the Island

Tahiti is a small tropical island in the pacific

Island Size

The island has an area of less than 500 square miles.

Varied Terrain

The terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lust tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano.

Population

Tahiti has an indigenous population of about 20,000.

Economy

Until a recent increase in tourism, most of the Tahiti economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.
